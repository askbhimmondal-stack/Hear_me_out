# 🎧 Hear Me Out

**Hear Me Out** is a modern web-based **YouTube to MP3 audio player** that allows users to listen to audio from YouTube videos by simply pasting a YouTube link. Instead of watching the full video, users can enjoy a **clean MP3-style music listening experience directly in the browser**.

The application is designed with a **minimal, fast, and distraction-free interface**, making it perfect for users who only want to listen to music, podcasts, or audio content from YouTube.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🎵 **Paste & Play** – Paste any YouTube video link and instantly play the audio  
- ▶️ **MP3 Style Player** – Simple and clean music player interface  
- ⏯️ **Playback Controls** – Play, pause, seek, and replay functionality  
- 🔊 **Volume Control** – Adjust audio volume easily  
- 📊 **Progress Bar** – Track playback time and progress  
- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight** – Optimized for quick loading and smooth playback  
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices  
- 🎧 **Distraction-Free Listening** – Focus only on audio without video

---

## 🖥️ How It Works

1. Copy a **YouTube video URL**.
2. Paste the link into the **Hear Me Out input box**.
3. Click the **Play button**.
4. The player extracts and plays the **audio stream** in a music player interface.
